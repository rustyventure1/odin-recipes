The Perfect Chocolate Chip Cookies

Indulge in the ultimate chocolate chip cookie experience with this mouthwatering recipe, featuring a golden-brown exterior and a soft, chewy center.

Each cookie is packed with rich, melty chocolate chips that create a delightful contrast with the buttery dough.

A hint of vanilla and a sprinkle of sea salt elevate the flavors, making every bite irresistibly delicious.

Perfectly baked and best enjoyed warm, these cookies will quickly become your go-to treat for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup butter
  • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2 tsp vanilla extract
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 kosher salt
  • 1 cup semisweet chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F.
  2. Microwave the butter for about 40 seconds. Butter should be completely melted but shouldnâ€™t be hot.
  3. In a large bowl, mix butter with the sugars until well-combined.
  4. Stir in vanilla and egg until incorporated.
  5. Add the flour, baking soda, and salt. Please read the recipe note about properly measuring flour.
  6. Mix dough until just combined. Dough should be soft and a little sticky but not overly sticky
  7. Stir in chocolate chips.
  8. Scoop out 1.5 tablespoons of dough (medium cookie scoop) and place 2 inches apart on baking sheet.
  9. Bake for 7-10 minutes, or until cookies are set. They will be puffy and still look a little underbaked in the middle.
  10. Let cool for 5 minutes and garnish with flaky sea salt
